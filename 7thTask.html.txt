    <h3>Ingredients</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>3 cups all-purpose flour</li>
        <li>3 cups granulated sugar</li>
        <li>1/2 cup cornstarch</li>
        <li>1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder</li>
        <li>1 tablespoon baking soda</li>
        <li>1½ teaspoons baking powder</li>
        <li>1½ teaspoons salt</li>
        <li>4 large eggs</li>
        <li>1½ cups buttermilk</li>
        <li>1¼ cups warm water</li>
        <li>1/2 cup vegetable oil</li>
        <li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
        <li>1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar</li>
        <li>2 tablespoons red food coloring</li>
    </ul>
    
        <h3>INSTRUCTIONS</h3>
    <ol>
        <li>Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter three 9-inch cake rounds. Dust with flour and tap out the excess.</li>
        <li>Mix together flour, sugar, cornstarch, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a stand mixer using a low speed until combined.</li>
        <li>Add eggs, buttermilk, warm water, oil, vanilla, vinegar, and food coloring. Beat on a medium speed until smooth. This should take just a couple of minutes.</li>
        <li>Divide batter among the three prepared pans.</li>
        <li>Bake for 30-35 minutes until the cake meets the toothpick test (stick a toothpick in and it comes out clean).</li>
        <li>Cool on wire racks for 15 minutes and then turn out the cakes onto the racks and allow to cool completely before frosting.</li>
        <li>Make the frosting. In a large bowl, beat together butter and cream cheese until fluffy. Use a hand mixer or stand mixer for best results. Add in vanilla extract. Beat until combined. Beat in powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time until frosting is smooth.</li>
        <li>Assemble and frost the completely cooled cake.</li>
    </ol>
